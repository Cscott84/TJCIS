Who We Are

The Jockey Club Information Systems, Inc. (TJCIS) is a unique and diversified organization that provides instant access to information and expertise to aid decision-making and improve Thoroughbred management and promotion. TJCIS is a wholly owned subsidiary of The Jockey Club and reinvests all profits in the Thoroughbred industry. TJCIS also owns a majority a interest in the The BloodHorse Magazine.

TJCIS Services & Solutions

Equineline.com

The global source for Thoroughbred and American Quarter Horse pedigrees and race records, as well as the innovative Portfolio Service, Trainer Program and Farm Program management services

Software and Consulting

Leading-edge applications for the equine industry, including Horse Farm Management and Veterinary management™ and web site and custom software development.

Catalogue Services

The premier research and production source of Thoroughbred sales catalogue pages for national and regional sales, as well as related products such as Pocket Pedigrees.

Additional Services

Unique services that integreate our comprehensive glodal database and technological expertise, inlcuding tattoo research for Thoroughbred indentification, customized reports and catalogue updates.
